Full paper: 15 minutes presentation followed by 5 minutes of Q&A

Short Paper: 10 minutes presentation followed by 5 minutes of Q&A

Poster: 5 minutes presentation without Q&A in the poster session (All posters will appear on poster boards in the poster area and questions will be answered by authors during poster exhibition)
